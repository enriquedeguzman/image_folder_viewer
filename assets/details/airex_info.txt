Contents Cefalexin monohydrate

Indications/Uses Urinary tract, resp tract, otitis media, skin
infections & other infections due to sensitive organisms.

Dosage/Direction for Use Adult 1-2 g daily given in divided doses 6-8 or
12 hrly intervals. Severe infections up to 6 g daily. Child 25-100 mg/kg
daily in divided doses up to max: 4 g daily. Prophylaxis of recurrent
UTI 125 mg at night.

Administration May be taken with or without food. May be taken w/ meals
to reduce GI discomfort.

Contraindications Hypersensitivity to cefalexin or other cephalosporins.

Special Precautions Allergic patients especially history of penicillin
allergy. Severe renal impairment. 

Adverse Reactions GI discomfort, diarrhea, allergic reactions eg, skin
rashes, urticaria, eosinophilia, angioedema, anaphylaxis; rises in liver
enzyme value.

Drug Interactions Bacteriostatic antibiotics. Probenecid.
