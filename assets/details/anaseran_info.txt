Contents Paracetamol

Indications/Uses Lowering of fever accompanying flu, common colds,
tonsillitis, measles, pneumonia & other infections. Relief of headache,
dysmenorrhea, rheumatism, arthritis & other muscle skeletal disorder.
Used for short-term treatment of moderate pain, particularly after
surgery & of fever.

Dosage/Direction for Use Tab Adult Mild cases 1 tab tid-qid. Moderate to
severe cases 2 tab tid-qid. Syr Child 7-12 yr 5-10 mL, 2-6 yr 2.5-5 mL,
3-6 yr 10 mL tid-qid, 1-3 yr 5 mL tid-qid. Infants 2.5 mL tid-qid. Drops
Children 1-2 yr 1.2-1.8 mL. Infant 6 mth-1 yr 0.6-1.2 mL, <6 mth 0.3-0.6
mL. To be taken every 4-6 hr. IV infusion for over 15 min. Patient >50
kg 1 g as single dose every 4 or more hr. Max: 4 g daily, 33-50 kg 15
mg/kg as single dose every 4 or more hr. Max: 60 mg/kg or 3 g daily.
Overdosage View Anaseran overdosage for action to be taken in the event
of an overdose.

Administration May be taken with or without food.

Contraindications Hypersensitivity.

Special Precautions Impaired kidney or liver function. 

Adverse Reactions Thrombocytopenia, leucopenia, pancytopenia,
neutropenia, agranulocytosis; severe liver damage (overdosage). 

Drug Interactions Increased toxicity with other potentially hepatotoxic
drugs or liver microsomal enzyme inducers. May accelerate absorption w/
metoclopramide. Altered plasma conc & excretion w/ probenecid. Reduced
absorption w/ colestyramine. Click to view Anaseran detailed prescribing
information
